Vladimir Dvorkin

Postdoctoral Researcher

Massachusetts Institute of Technology

Biography

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, affiliated with Energy Initiative and Laboratory for Information and Decision Systems. My research interests are at the interfaces of algorithmic (differential) privacy, stochastic optimization & control, and economics. Before joining MIT, I pursued a Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering at the Technical University of Denmark in 2017-2020. In 2019, I was a research visitor at the School of Industrial and Systems Engineering of Georgia Tech. My current research is focused on performance guarantees in multi-stage stochastic optimization, privacy algorithms, and optimization for machine learning tasks. In 2021, I co-organized enOPTIMAL seminar series to promote research exchange during the pandemic.
